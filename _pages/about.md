Hi, I'm Jacob, a Computer Science PhD student at [Imperial College London](https://www.imperial.ac.uk/computing/) advised by [Yingzhen Li](http://yingzhenli.net/home/en/) and [Marek Rei](https://www.marekrei.com/). My research interests span various domains including generative<sup>[1](https://arxiv.org/abs/2406.00426) [2](https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.04798)</sup> and language<sup>[1](https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.02327) [2](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.06582)</sup> models as well as reinforcement learning<sup>[1](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.12229)</sup>. Currently, I am a Research Intern at [Epic Games](https://www.epicgames.com/site/home). Previously, I was a Research Intern at [Samsung AI Research](https://research.samsung.com/) (2025).

Before joining Imperial, I grew up in Singapore and Malaysia, then earned my Bachelor's from the [University of Toronto](https://www.utoronto.ca/) and my Master's from the [University of California, Los Angeles](https://www.ucla.edu/). I was fortunate to be advised by [Rahul Krishnan](http://www.cs.toronto.edu/~rahulgk/index.html) at the [Vector Institute](https://vectorinstitute.ai/), and [Jonathan Kao](https://seas.ucla.edu/~kao/people_dir/jonathan_kao.html) at [UCLA SEAS](https://samueli.ucla.edu/). Lastly, I am grateful to my instructors, [Jimmy Ba](https://jimmylba.github.io/) and [David Duvenaud](https://www.cs.toronto.edu/~duvenaud/), for sparking my initial interest in machine learning.
